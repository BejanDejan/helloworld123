A simple music player by nobsutils.com
 
Click ⏏ or press o or drag&drop to add music files to the playlist. 
Files are never transferred over the internet or uploaded to the cloud.

Keyboard shortcuts:

  z                    play previous
  x or enter           play selected
  c                    pause/resume
  v                    stop
  b                    play next

  left/right           seek 2s back/forward 
  0..9                 seek to 0%..90%  
 
  s                    toggle shuffle
  r                    repeat none/all/one 
  t                    toggle elapsed/remaining time display

  o                    open dialog to add files
  del                  remove items from the list
  up/down              navigate the list
  ctrl+shift+up/down   move items up/down the list
  f                    focus current in the list
 
  j or space           open search/queue

  alt+enter or q       toggle item in queue
  alt+up/down          reorder items in queue

  ctrl+c               copy selected to clipboard as text
 
  d                    toggle dark theme
  l                    google lyrics
  ?                    toggle this screen
  esc                  back to the list
